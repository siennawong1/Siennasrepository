WATCH! The Minority Report

In the movie there is a software that shows the scene and events of a murder before it takes places. The user can zoom in on images, change the angle that the scene is being viewed at, and hear the voices and sounds that are happening at the time. The user uses special gloves to manipulate, move and rotate the scene.

Teamlab Japan

An interactive gallery where visitors can interact with artworks through their senses. It is a fully digital immersive art experience and can make the visitor feel truly connected to the artwork. I have always wanted to visit Teamlab as it looks like it would feel more like a social experience that you can share with others.
